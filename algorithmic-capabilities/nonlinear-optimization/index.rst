Non-Linear Optimization
==============================

.. meta::
   :description: Non-Linear Optimization in AIMMS solves problems with non-linear variable relationships, ideal for energy, pricing, and chemical modeling.
   :keywords: AIMMS Non-Linear Optimization, non-linear modeling AIMMS, AIMMS energy optimization, AIMMS pricing strategies, AIMMS chemical processes, non-linear relationships AIMMS, AIMMS advanced solvers, AIMMS non-linear equations, AIMMS robust optimization, AIMMS complex modeling.

**Non-Linear Optimization** in AIMMS is designed to solve problems with non-linear relationships between variables. 
It supports applications such as energy optimization, pricing strategies, and chemical process modeling, offering robust tools to handle 
complex and non-linear interactions.
